Topscore Recruitment Workflow

Candidate request (mid-level positions) will be processed within 7-14 days except for senior level positions.

  1. Client Briefing & Role Definition- The client will provide details such as; the duties and responsibilities of the candidate, skills, qualifications, authorizations, and professional memberships required of the candidate, job location and hours of work, monthly gross remuneration, other benefits and any other information which the client deems relevant to the role, or as reasonably requested by Topscore consultants.

  2. Candidate Sourcing (ads, visits, social media)- Upon receipt of a request from the client, Topscore Co. will clarify the job description and role requirements, and agree on a timeline for the recruitment with the client then advertise the role (online and offline).

  3. Shortlisting & Screening- Topscore will shortlist, interview, and forward details of shortlisted Candidates to the Client within 5-7working days.

  4. Client Interviews- Schedule interviews of the qualified Candidates with the Client.

  5. Training Preparation- All Topscore candidates are to be trained prior to placement to ensure they are prepared and equipped with skill set to help them fit and deliver results as expected.

  6. Final Placement & Onboarding- Where applicable, Topscore will also help with onboarding of the candidates through a customized orientation and onboarding program.
